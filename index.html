Events

Navyug

"Navyug" is the vibrant annual fest of Zarurat, the social arm of JECRC. It has evolved into a crucial event for every member of Team Zarurat over the past decade. Last year's Navyug was a standout moment, recognized as one of the best events of the year. A blend of cultural and sports events, topped by heartwarming goodies distribution, brought joy to over 200 faces.

Diya Exhibition

Zarurat's Diya Exhibition is a heartwarming event where participants paint and sell creatively adorned diyas. The proceeds support underprivileged communities, funding essential resources and promoting artistic expression. It's a showcase of talent and compassion, brightening homes and lives alike.

Vastra Samman Samaroh

"Vastra Samman Samaroh" is a compassionate event where volunteers collect and redistribute gently used clothing to the underprivileged. Conducted to provide clothing to those in need, it embodies the spirit of giving and community support.

Our Misson

Abhyudaya Zarurat, an NGO initiated by students at JECRC University, is committed to brightening the futures of underprivileged children with the motto "The Help Beyond."
Focused solely on education, the foundation provides free education programs to bridge the educational gap. Through dedicated efforts, Abhyudaya Zarurat aims to
empower underprivileged children with the knowledge and skills necessary for a brighter future, all at the cost of their smiles. The foundation's mission is to make a
positive and enduring impact on the lives of these children through the collective dedication of passionate students.
